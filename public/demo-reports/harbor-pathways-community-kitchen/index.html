Organization: Harbor Pathways Community Kitchen

Prepared by: connectNPO

Human review status: Required before sharing externally

Executive Summary

Harbor Pathways Community Kitchen appears to have a strong service foundation, especially around weekly meals, pantry referrals, volunteer meal prep, and limited delivery coordination. The main strategic focus should be donor readiness: the organization wants more recurring donors, but the intake says monthly gift impact is not yet clearly explained. Before investing heavily in SEO, broad grant outreach, or larger marketing campaigns, Harbor Pathways should organize its donor message, program outcomes, and basic follow-up workflows. This will make the website clearer, make giving easier to understand, and prepare stronger material for future grant conversations.
